For Filmmakers

AI Filmmaker Inquiries

PEP is seeking collaborators with demonstrated expertise in AI-driven filmmaking — directors, generative artists, and post-production specialists capable of producing theatrical-quality work. This is not an open call for those learning the tools.

Who We're Looking For

  • AI directors with proven generative pipelines and a feature-quality reel.
  • Generative artists producing shot-ready imagery and motion at a professional standard.
  • AI post-production specialists — rotoscoping, upscaling, retiming, VFX, voice, restoration.
  • Pipeline engineers building reproducible, controllable AI workflows for narrative work.

What We Expect From Your Reel

  • Theatrical-quality finished work. Not test renders, not tutorials, not "look what I made yesterday."
  • Demonstrated control over output — consistency, intentional direction, repeatability.
  • A clear breakdown of your role and your pipeline on at least one piece.

How to Submit

Email your inquiry directly to PEP. One complete email per inquiry.

Inquiries
contact@potatoeaterproductions.com

Email Format

  • Subject: AI Filmmaker Inquiry — [Your Name]
  • Body: Name, location, role(s), tools you work in, availability and rate structure.
  • Reel: Public link to your work — Vimeo, YouTube, IMDb, or your own portfolio site only.
  • Pipeline breakdown: For one piece in your reel, describe the tools used, your role, and the time invested.
  • Acknowledgment: Include the line "I have read and agree to the Submission Acknowledgment."

Reel link requirements. Public hosting only — Vimeo, YouTube, IMDb, or a personal portfolio site at your own domain. Inquiries with links to Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, or other private file-sharing services will not be opened.

A direct inquiry form will be added to this page in the future. Until then, please use email.

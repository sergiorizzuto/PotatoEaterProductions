For Filmmakers

AI Filmmaker Inquiries

We are looking for the best AI filmmakers to make feature films, with shorts also considered. Demonstrated theatrical-quality work required. This is not an open call for those learning the tools.

Who We're Looking For

  • AI directors with proven generative pipelines and a feature-quality reel.
  • Generative artists producing shot-ready imagery and motion at a professional standard.
  • AI post-production specialists in VFX, restoration, retiming, upscaling, and voice.

How to Inquire

Email a single, complete inquiry.

Inquiries
contact@potatoeaterproductions.com

Include in Your Email

  • Subject: AI Filmmaker Inquiry — [Your Name]
  • About you: Name, location, role, tools you work in, availability and rate.
  • Reel link: Vimeo, YouTube, IMDb, or your portfolio site only.
  • Pipeline note: For one piece in your reel, describe your role, the tools, and time invested.
  • Acknowledgment: Include the line "I have read and agree to the Submission Acknowledgment at potatoeaterproductions.com/releases."

Reel link requirements. Vimeo, YouTube, IMDb, or a personal portfolio site at your own domain only. Inquiries with Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, or other private file-share links will not be opened.

By inquiring, you agree to the Submission Acknowledgment.

A direct inquiry form will be added to this page in the future. Until then, please use email.

AI Filmmaker Inquiries

We are looking for the best AI filmmakers in the world to make feature films.

Potato Eater Productions is actively seeking directors and generative artists capable of producing theatrical-quality feature films using AI pipelines. Short-form work is also welcome, but our primary focus is the feature.

This is not an open call for those still learning the tools. If you are doing serious work and have a reel that proves it, we want to hear from you.

Send Us Your Reel

Email a link to your strongest finished work, a one-paragraph note on your pipeline and creative role, and a sentence on what kind of feature you would want to make.

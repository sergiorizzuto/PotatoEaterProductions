For Writers

Submit a Script

Feature-length screenplays from writers with a perspective and the craft to back it up.

What We're Looking For

  • Feature-length screenplays (roughly 90–120 pages).
  • Stories with substance and a clear point of view. —
  • Originals preferred; well-conceived adaptations considered if you control the rights.
  • We are open to most genres. We do not review horror.

Format

Required

  • Length Feature-length, approximately 90–120 pages.
  • Format Industry-standard screenplay format. PDF only.
  • Logline Clear and concise. In the body of the email.
  • Title page Title, writer(s), contact information, draft date.

How to Submit

Email your inquiry directly. In the email, include a two-sentence logline and a short paragraph about why this story matters to you. Attach the script as a PDF. Do not send via shared cloud links unless we ask for them.

Send Submissions To
submissions@potatoeaterproductions.com

Before you submit: by submitting any material to Potato Eater Productions, you affirmatively acknowledge that you have read and agree to be bound by our Submission Release and Agreement. Do not submit if you do not agree.

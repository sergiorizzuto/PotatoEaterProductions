For Writers

Script Submissions

Feature screenplays only. Complete drafts. Read the guidelines below before submitting.

What We're Looking For

  • Feature-length screenplays only. No shorts, no treatments, no outlines.
  • Complete drafts only. Works in progress will not be reviewed.
  • Open to most genres.
  • Sub-$10M budget tier. Realistic, producible scopes.
  • Stories with something to say. We're drawn to material that earns its runtime — honest, well-crafted scripts about freedom, justice, integrity, grit, and the lives of people doing real work.
  • Spec or further along. Packaged projects with attached talent or financing are welcome but not required.

What We're Not Looking For

  • Horror in any form.
  • Spectacle, violence, or action without purpose.
  • Incomplete drafts, treatments, or pitches without a script attached.
  • Material requiring major IP licensing or budgets above $10M.

How to Submit

Email your submission directly. One complete email per submission.

Submissions
contact@potatoeaterproductions.com

Email Format

  • Subject: Script Submission — [Title]
  • Body: Name, contact info, logline, genre, page count, status (spec / optioned / packaged), one-paragraph synopsis, brief note on why this script belongs at Potato Eater Productions.
  • Attachment: Complete screenplay as PDF only.
  • Acknowledgment: Include the line "I have read and agree to the Submission Release at potatoeaterproductions.com/releases."

One submission per email. Do not send scripts across multiple messages. Submissions missing required information or sent in multiple pieces will not be reviewed.

Before submitting, read the full Submission Release and Agreement. By submitting, you agree to its terms.

A direct submission form will be added to this page in the future. Until then, please use email.
